Excercise 6

The reasons to avoid having each teacher write their own style is to avoid having clashing styles when moving from one page to another. If everyone has the same styles the website will be more uniform and easy to follow. Imagine having a website where the navigation bar is at the top on one page and then on the left side on another page. It doesn't make sense when trying to navigate.

some tags that could be used ul and li's will make for a easy nav bar, the styling for that will depend where the placement of the nav bar is, considering standard webpages today they have the nav bar in a hamburger icon and it pops up on the side. Other tags used will be h1 - h6 to denote different sections of a page. These can easily be stlyed for the particular course using different colors to denote different courses. Also the content of the pages will need to be housed in a p tag and can be styled or centered. Table, tr, td and th can be used for grades and student id or even a course schedule.
